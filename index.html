Bio

Hi! I'm a second-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at Santa Clara University, working under the guidance of Professor Yi Fang. Prior to joining SCU, I completed my undergraduate degree in Physics at the University of California, Davis. I'm currently on track to graduate in 2028.

Research

My research focus is on the potential and challenges of leveraging LLMs for Information Retrieval, particularly in reasoning-based LLM reranking, RAG, and synthetic training data generation for IR systems.
